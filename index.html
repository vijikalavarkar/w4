4W of My Life

A journey through the four essential questions that define our human connections

Who Borned You

The Foundation

Those who brought you into this world, nurtured your first breath, and laid the foundation of your existence. The eternal gratitude we owe to our creators - our parents, guardians, and the loving hands that shaped our earliest days.

Who is Born with You

The Companions

The souls who walk alongside you through life's journey. Family, friends, siblings, and kindred spirits who share your path, understand your story, and create the beautiful tapestry of shared experiences.

Who is Born for You

The Destined

The special someone meant to complete your story. Your soulmate, life partner, or that one person who changes everything and makes life meaningful. The connection that feels written in the stars.

Who is Born from You

The Legacy

The next generation that carries your essence forward. Your children, students, mentees, or those whose lives you've shaped and who will continue your story long after you're gone. Your living legacy.

The Circle of Life

These four connections form the complete tapestry of human existence. From those who gave us life, to those who share our journey, to those destined for us, and finally to those who carry us forward. Understanding these relationships helps us appreciate the beautiful interconnectedness of our lives.
